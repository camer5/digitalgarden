# Maralyn daedrith 
![Pasted image 20260122201949.png|right|300](/img/user/Images/Pasted%20image%2020260122201949.png)

***

Maralyn daedrith is a member of [[AA Player links/The Martyrs\|The Martyrs]] guild with the rank of [[AA Player links/Commander\|Commander]]. They are a 256 year old elven woman who doesnt remeber much of thier childhood and for as long as they can remeber now has been serving under [[AA Player links/The Martyrs\|The Martyrs]]. 
They have pale brown skin and ragged short black hair, thier skin is covered in many different scars and wounds gained from battle and war over the years and as such has lost the functionality of thier right ear with most of it being bitten off.  They wield a longsword but are no stranger to magic yet thier techniques are often kept hidden from the public.

The first time the party met this individual is when ther were imprisoned for the death of [[Npcs/Aquilan wyntris\|Aquilan wyntris]].
